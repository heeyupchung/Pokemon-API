About

Random Team

If you are looking for a team of diverse random pokemon to run through Pokemon Brilliant Diamond or Shining Pearl with, then click the "Random Team" button above to generate your new team.

Explore the Sinnoh region with randomized pokemon for a fresh, new experience!

Monotype Team

Want to run through Brilliant Diamond or Shining Pearl like a gym leader? Click on the "Monotype Team" button above to generate a pokemon team where each pokemon is the same type for the faithful remake of Diamond and Pearl.

Experience the challenges and joys of running a monotype team in the Sinnoh region!
